Welcome to Fired Arts Studio

Welcome to Fired Arts Studio, Kokomo's premier paint-your-own-pottery and creative arts center! We believe in the transformative power of art and provide a welcoming space for everyone, from curious beginners to seasoned artists, to explore their creativity.

Founded with a passion for art and community, we offer a wide selection of pre-fired bisque (as you can see!), engaging workshops for pottery and canvas painting, and open studio time. Our experienced and friendly staff are dedicated to guiding you through your artistic journey.

Come join our vibrant community, get your hands dirty with clay or pick up a brush for canvas, and create something beautiful and unique!
